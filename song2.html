Song 2 – MY EYES MY SOUL

Creation Timeline

Beat Process & Inspirations

The creation of the beat came from playing around with certain synths, chopping them up and bit-crushing it, creating the main lead. Followed by that comes beautiful electric piano chords, making the track have that calm-lofi aesthetic. The piano was also paired with airy strings to create a richer, heavenly-like texture. Next came the hard trap drums paired with 808's to blend trap and lo-fi together creating another etheral beat, fit for this EP.

MY EYES MY SOUL Instrumental

Lyric Process & Inspirations

The lyrics, as well as the sound of the track was heavenly inspired by A$AP Rocky with his use of pitched down vocals, which was used for the chorus to create a cloud rap, dream-like aesthetic. The delivery and cadence of the verses were also influenced by Denzel Curry with his use of triplet flows. The contents of the lyrics reflect me wanting to "blow smoke" as in clearing my head and wondering why I'm even worrying about the things I'm worrying about. The lyrics continue to have a brag-like tone to them, with a confident delivery reflecting how I consider my craft to be of quality, unlike certain rappers already in the industry.

Recording, Mixing & Mastering

The mixing and mastering here utilised me pitching down the vocals for the chorus to create that spacious psychedlic vibe and also used compressors, reverb, delay and EQ.
