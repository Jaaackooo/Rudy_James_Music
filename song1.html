Song 1 – DUM DUM

Creation Timeline

Beat Process & Inspirations

The instrumental of DUM DUM came about when wanting to create an etheral, yet hard hitting beat. I wanted airy sounds like synth pads and bell-like electric pianos on top of deep 808's and hard trap drums to create a cloud-rap like beat.

DUM DUM Instrumental

Lyric Process & Inspirations

The lyrics and flow was inspired by Artist's like Kendrick Lamar with a fast paced lyrical flow. The contents of the lyrics reflect a sense of pride and has a braggadocious tone throughout where I rap as if I'm "better" than most new gen artists.

Demos

Here's an early recording Iphone recording of the song.

Recording, Mixing & Mastering

The mixing and mastering was kept simple, only using compressors, reverb, delay and EQ
