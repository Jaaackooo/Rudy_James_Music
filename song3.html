Song 3 – OUSTIDE

Creation Timeline

Beat Process & Inspirations

The creation of this beat came from experimenting with etheral pads, where I played them as chords and chopped them like stabs, creating a dream-like aesthetic. It also reminds me a bit of the beat from Travis Scott's song CAROUSEL, where in both beats achieved a psychedelic trap sound. After that, I played a soft electric piano melody and added strings to further add to the psychedelic feel to the song. I then added the drums and 808s to complete the beat.

OUTSIDE Instrumental

Lyric Process & Inspirations

For this song I decided I wanted to follow a fictional story about somebody being with a girl that already had a relationship. The lyrics also focuses on the concept of fake and decietful individuals and plays into a part where her actual lover found out and now the girl has to deal with the consequences of her actions. The rapping in this track also features a laid-back style where I float over the beat to create a chill, yet groovy vibe.

Demos

Listen to rough demos (beats only and beats with lyrics).

Recording, Mixing & Mastering

The mixing and mastering featured compressors, EQ, reverb and delay to create a clean mix.
