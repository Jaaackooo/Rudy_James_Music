Song 4 – TIT FOR TAT

Song Creation Timeline

Beat Process & Inspirations

This is the only beat that wasn't made by me, but instead was made by my frequent collaborator mai mai also known as the other half of DOJIMA, where the beat has that calm, lo-fi trap feel that fits nicely into the rest of the EP.

TIT FOR TAT Instrumental

Lyric Process & Inspirations

For this song the lyrics refer to me trying to compare with other rappers, saying that whatever they do, I do it better, and also speaks about me not caring about people trying to flex their wealth. It also contains fast-paced rapping, inspired by Kendrick Lamar performed with a similar cadence to him, and even has a little reference to him when I say "Training tryn be like Kung Fu Kenny."

Demo

Mixing & Mastering Proccess

The mixing and mastering here featured compressors, EQ, reverb, delay and also panned tracks for the ad-libs to create a cool, spacious vibe.
